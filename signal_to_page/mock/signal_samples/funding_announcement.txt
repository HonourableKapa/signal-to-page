Acme RevOps Raises $40M Series B to Scale AI-Powered Revenue Operations Platform

SAN FRANCISCO, CA — Acme RevOps, the leading revenue operations platform for mid-market B2B companies, today announced it has raised $40 million in Series B funding led by Sequoia Capital, with participation from existing investors Bessemer Venture Partners and Salesforce Ventures.

The company will use the proceeds to accelerate product development, expand its go-to-market team, and launch into European markets. Acme RevOps currently serves over 800 customers including Datadog, Lattice, and Gong.

"Revenue teams are drowning in disconnected tools and spreadsheets," said Jane Smith, CEO of Acme RevOps. "Our platform brings together forecasting, pipeline management, and sales analytics into a single source of truth."

The company plans to grow from 120 to 250 employees by end of year, with a focus on engineering, sales, and customer success hires.

About Acme RevOps
Acme RevOps was founded in 2020 and is headquartered in San Francisco. The company helps revenue teams at mid-market B2B companies streamline operations, improve forecast accuracy, and drive predictable growth.
